## AI-Powered Travel App

The project is an AI-powered travel application designed to make trip planning, booking, budgeting, and travel management easier within a single platform. Instead of using different apps for planning itineraries, comparing transport options, booking tickets, splitting expenses, and checking safety, users can manage their complete journey through one intelligent travel assistant.

The app creates personalized travel plans based on the user’s destination, budget, travel dates, interests, group size, and preferred comfort level. It recommends the best way to travel by comparing options such as buses, trains, and flights based on price, travel time, and convenience.

### Main Features

1. **AI Trip Planner**
   Creates a complete day-by-day itinerary based on the user’s budget, interests, duration, and destination.

2. **Smart Journey**
   Compares bus, train, flight, and other transport options and recommends the best choice based on cost, comfort, and travel time.

3. **Smart Ticket Finder**
   Allows users to search for and book bus, train, and flight tickets directly through the app.

4. **Split Expense Feature**
   Helps groups record shared expenses, calculate individual contributions, and settle payments.

5. **Cheapest Flight Finder**
   Searches different airlines and travel dates to identify affordable flight options.

6. **Safety Mode**
   Provides emergency contacts, live-location sharing, safety alerts, risky-area warnings, and nearby emergency services.

7. **Budget-Based Discovery**
   Suggests destinations, hotels, attractions, restaurants, and activities that match the user’s available budget.

8. **AI Recommended Packing Checklist**
   Creates a personalized packing list based on the destination, weather, trip duration, planned activities, and travel type.

9. **Hidden Cost Calculator**
   Estimates additional costs such as taxes, baggage charges, local transportation, food, entry fees, and service charges.

10. **Price Drop Alerts**
    Notifies users when prices decrease for saved flights, hotels, or other travel bookings.

### Project Goal

The goal of the project is to reduce the stress, confusion, and time involved in planning a trip. The app helps users make better travel decisions by providing accurate comparisons, personalized recommendations, transparent cost estimates, and safety support.

### Target Users

The app is suitable for solo travellers, students, families, couples, business travellers, and groups of friends. It is especially useful for budget-conscious users who want to plan affordable and well-organized trips.

### Unique Value

The main advantage of the app is that it combines trip planning, transport comparison, ticket booking, expense management, safety features, packing assistance, and price tracking in one platform. The AI continuously assists the user before, during, and after the journey, making the entire travel experience simpler, safer, and more personalized.
